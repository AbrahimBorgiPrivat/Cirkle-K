Article 05 / Results

Results

The final outcome is a compact pair of business cases: one focused sales analysis and one broader loyalty simulation with reusable technical structure.

Outcome at a glance

  • The interview case ends in concrete findings about sales lift, margin, and promotion timing.
  • The simulation case ends in a broader loyalty story around segments, campaigns, and station behavior.
  • The shared result is a delivery that links data work, semantic design, and decision-ready reporting.

Interview findings

  • Sales increased about `28%` and margin about `16%` in 2018 versus 2017.
  • The promotion mainly increased average units per transaction.
  • Station 1 and Station 3 showed the strongest promotion effect.
  • The most visible uplift period was `24 July` to `4 August`.

Simulation findings

  • The first half of 2025 simulation generated about `474,000` transactions and about `DKK 76 million` revenue.
  • The highest station activity was concentrated in Copenhagen, Fyn, and Sjaelland.
  • Fuel-Only Driver had the most sign-ups.
  • Heavy Shopper showed the highest long-term value.
  • Red Bull and Faxe Kondi Booster campaigns were triggered most often.

Delivery outcome

  • Dockerized ETL flows and PBIP workspaces are versioned in one repository.
  • Tabular Editor automation is prepared for both PBIP models.
  • The site is ready to be published as a static GitHub Pages project.
Short summary

The solution is strongest as a combined story: technical structure, usable semantic models, and clear business-facing Power BI output.
