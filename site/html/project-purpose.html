Article 01 / Purpose

Purpose

The two cases were built to show both focused business analysis and broader end-to-end analytics engineering.

Interview analysis Simulation pipeline Power BI storytelling

Purpose in short

  • The interview case focuses on a concrete business question around sales, promotion effect, and site comparison.
  • The simulation case expands the scope into a full analytics delivery with synthetic data, PostgreSQL, and reporting.
  • Together they show both business reasoning and technical execution in one solution.

Interview case

  • Analyze ice-cream sales for Danish sites across 2017 and 2018.
  • Measure the effect of the 2018 promotion, identified by `trn_promotion_id = 47025`.
  • Design three pages: overview, compare sites, and driver tree.

Simulation case

  • Show how a realistic Circle K loyalty environment can be simulated end to end.
  • Model customers, cards, stations, transactions, campaigns, and geography.
  • Use the result to answer practical questions about segments, LTV, and campaign engagement.

What the solution is meant to demonstrate

  • Business-facing interpretation, not just visuals.
  • Reusable ETL and semantic-model structure.
  • Ability to move from raw input to a report that is easy to explain.
