Article 04 / Power BI

Power BI Report Pages

The interview case contributes three focused analytical pages. The simulation case contributes three broader loyalty-reporting pages. Together they form a 2x3 overview.

Reading guide

  • The interview pages focus on change over time, site comparison, and KPI drivers.
  • The simulation pages focus on program scale, customer segments, and campaign behavior.
  • Each page is designed to answer one business question quickly rather than cover everything at once.
Interview

Overview

KPIs, trends, SPLY, top products, and flexible breakdowns.

Interview

Compare Sites

A/B comparison of site groups with gaps, p-values, and significance.

Interview

Driver Tree

Top-down KPI decomposition into sales, expenses, units, and price.

Simulation

Overview

Program footprint, station activity, revenue, and time patterns.

Simulation

Segmentation

Sign-up patterns, segment revenue, and long-term value differences.

Simulation

Campaigns

Reward usage, campaign triggers, and segment or region engagement.

Interview report logic

  • Overview answers what changed between the two years.
  • Compare Sites supports targeted station benchmarking.
  • Driver Tree makes the promotion effect easier to explain.

Simulation report logic

  • Overview tracks loyalty-program scale and concentration.
  • Segmentation highlights value differences between customer types.
  • Campaigns shows which mechanics and groups drive engagement.
Format

The pages are kept short and visual on purpose, with one main story per tile.
